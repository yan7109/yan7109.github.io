Founder, CTO, software engineer.

I’m a founder and technologist working at the intersection of artificial intelligence, product design, and creative software. I co-founded Polarr, where I served as CTO and built AI-powered photo and video tools used by millions, and shipped computer vision systems across mobile, desktop, and embedded platforms (Samsung, LG, Xiaomi, OPPO, Lenovo, and others).

My work spans machine learning, computer vision, edge inference, developer platforms, and human-centered product design. I care deeply about building AI tools that save people time, reduce friction, and expand creative freedom.

In May 2025, Polarr was acquired by Pixieset, where I now serve as Director of Engineering. Alongside this, I take photos of my family and friends, advise founders on startup exits and M&A, and experiment with new foundation models.

You can find me on LinkedIn, view my photography at derekyan.pixieset.com, or email me at derek@polarr.co.
