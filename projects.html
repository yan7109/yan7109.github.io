Polarr Next

Professional photo culling and editing, entirely in the browser, fully offline. Photographers edit a few reference photos and the engine learns their style, then applies it across thousands of images — reducing post-processing time by up to 90%. Runs deep learning models on-device via WebGPU, WebGL, and WebAssembly. No uploads, no cloud.

The proudest thing I've built.
