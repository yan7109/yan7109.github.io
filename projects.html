Polarr

I co-founded Polarr and served as CTO from inception through acquisition in 2025. We built AI-powered photo and video editing tools, scaling to 6M monthly active users across iOS, Android, Mac, Windows, and web. I led the engineering team in shipping on-device computer vision and model compression technology that ran on 100M+ devices, including embedded partnerships with Samsung, LG, Western Digital, Xiaomi, OPPO, and Lenovo. We raised $13.5M from DFJ and other investors.
